Animating Vancouver's Watersheds

Stream Networks of the Capilano, Seymour, and Coquitlam Reservoirs

Give it a second to load! This animation was inspired by a 2021 blog post by Stamen Design. I hit a surprising number of dead ends - it turns out that live-rendering 17,000 line segments is a bit more memory-intensive than I anticipated. To remedy this, I reclassified the local DEM to 25-meter increments and performed a spatial join with the stream polylines in order to assign each line segment an elevation class. The stream network was then exported as a GeoJSON and drawn over a Leaflet map using P5.JS.
